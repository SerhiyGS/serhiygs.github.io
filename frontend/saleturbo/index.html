Important Success Tips

Leverage all our features to snag deals daily!

1
 Email alerts

In the race for eBay deals, every second counts.

Our alerts are designed to notify you as soon as a new listing is posted, if our algorithm marks it as a potential deal.

Pair them with an email app like Gmail that offers push notifications to get notified instantly.

Each alert contains a direct link, allowing you to jump on deals quickly and be the first to buy.

2
 Live feed

Stay updated with our Live Feed, a real-time stream of eBay listings that you can keep as an open tab in your browser.

Our live feed can show you every new iPhone listing, deal or not, and is fully customizable with your filters.

Ideal for desktop use, the Live Feed lets you keep track all recent listings while you're busy with other tasks. This way, you won't miss any opportunity, including listings that might not be flagged as deals by our algorithm.

3
 Browse all

Discover every iPhone listing on eBay, new or old, with our 'Browse All' search interface.

Use filters to refine your search by model, condition, price, and sellers open to offers.

This feature is particularly useful for thorough exploration, allowing you to find opportunities among both recent and older listings, including sellers who are open to negotiating prices.
